The Mission

Combining aerial scouting with ground-level operations for unparalleled exploration.

Core Idea

Our project integrates an autonomous drone and rover into a single, cohesive system. This platform is engineered for both terrestrial and extraterrestrial missions, combining the drone's aerial scouting capabilities with the rover's robust ground-level operations. The primary goal is to achieve efficient exploration, detailed mapping, and resource extraction in the most challenging terrains imaginable.

Technical Approach

Leveraging cutting-edge technology for autonomous operation.

System Architecture

Drone: Performs aerial mapping, terrain analysis, and obstacle detection.

Rover: Handles ground mobility, resource collection, and acts as a data relay.

Autonomy & AI

AI-driven navigation using SLAM (Simultaneous Localization & Mapping) is at the core of our system.

We utilize sensor fusion with LiDAR, cameras, IMUs, Radar, and more for robust environmental awareness.

Communication & Power

Drone-to-Rover coordination is managed via wireless mesh networks, ensuring constant contact.

The system is powered by solar charging stations and swappable batteries for long-duration missions.

  • Ultra-Light Design: Ingenuity weighs just 1.8 kg, utilizing a carbon fiber frame to minimize mass.
